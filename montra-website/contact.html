Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome! However, we recommend calling ahead to ensure availability, especially during peak hours (evenings and weekends).

What should I wear?

For Thai massage, we provide comfortable clothing. For oil massages (Swedish, Deep Tissue), most clients undress to their comfort level. You'll be draped with sheets throughout.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 5-10 minutes early for first visits to complete intake forms. This ensures your full massage time is not shortened.

Can I request a specific therapist?

Absolutely! If you have a preference, just let us know when booking. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee. Please call us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available in any amount and never expire. Perfect for any occasion. Call us to purchase.
