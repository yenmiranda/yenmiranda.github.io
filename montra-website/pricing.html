What is Cupping Therapy?

Cupping is an ancient form of alternative medicine that involves placing cups on the skin to create suction. This powerful therapy helps with pain relief, inflammation reduction, improved blood flow, and provides deep-tissue therapeutic benefits.

Benefits of Cupping:

  • Relieves muscle tension and pain
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Promotes deep relaxation
  • Enhances athletic recovery
