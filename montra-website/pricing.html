Pricing Questions?

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer first-time discounts?

Call us to ask about current promotions for new clients! We often have special offers available.

Can I use my HSA/FSA card?

Yes! Massage therapy is often covered by HSA/FSA accounts when prescribed by a doctor. Check with your provider.

Do you take insurance?

We provide receipts you can submit to your insurance. Some plans cover massage therapy with a prescription.

What about gratuity?

Tips are appreciated but never expected or required. Our therapists are paid fairly and your satisfaction is what matters most.

Can I split payment between two cards?

Absolutely! We can split payment across multiple cards or payment methods if needed.

Do packages expire?

Our packages have generous expiration periods (4-6 months), giving you plenty of time to use them at your convenience.
