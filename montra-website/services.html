Ancient healing through stretching and acupressure

Experience the ancient healing art combining acupressure, Indian Ayurvedic principles, and assisted yoga postures. Unlike typical massage therapy, you remain fully clothed as your therapist guides you through yoga-like stretches.

Key Benefits

  • Increases flexibility and range of motion
  • Relieves muscle tension and joint pain
  • Improves circulation and energy flow
  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation

What to Expect

Your session begins with a consultation about your wellness goals. You'll wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. The massage is performed on a mat on the floor, allowing for the full range of stretching movements that make Thai massage unique.

Prenatal Massage

Specialized care for expecting mothers

Massage therapy specifically adapted for pregnancy. Our certified therapists use proper positioning and techniques that are safe and beneficial for both mother and baby throughout all stages of pregnancy.

Key Benefits

  • Reduces back and joint pain
  • Decreases swelling in hands and feet
  • Relieves muscle tension and headaches
  • Reduces stress and improves sleep
Book This Service

What to Expect

We use specially designed cushions and side-lying positions for comfort and safety. Our therapists avoid certain pressure points and use gentle techniques appropriate for pregnancy.

Important: Please consult with your healthcare provider before booking, especially if you have any high-risk conditions. Recommended after first trimester.
