Traditional Thai Massage

Experience the ancient healing art that has been passed down through generations of Thai practitioners.

What is Traditional Thai Massage?

Traditional Thai massage is a unique healing system combining acupressure, Indian Ayurvedic principles, and assisted yoga postures. Unlike typical massage therapy, you remain fully clothed during the session as your therapist uses their hands, knees, legs, and feet to move you into a series of yoga-like stretches.

Benefits

  • Increases flexibility and range of motion
  • Relieves muscle tension and joint pain
  • Improves circulation and lymphatic drainage
  • Boosts energy levels
  • Reduces stress and promotes deep relaxation
  • Improves posture and body alignment

What to Expect

Your session begins with a brief consultation about your health and wellness goals. You'll wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing provided by us or your own. The massage is performed on a comfortable mat on the floor, allowing for the full range of stretching movements that make Thai massage unique.

Duration Options

60 Minutes Perfect for first-timers or targeting specific areas
90 Minutes Our most popular option for full-body treatment
120 Minutes Ultimate relaxation and therapeutic benefits

Deep Tissue Massage

Therapeutic massage that targets the deepest layers of muscle tissue, tendons, and fascia.

What is Deep Tissue Massage?

Deep tissue massage is a focused treatment that uses firm pressure and slow strokes to reach deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. This technique is particularly effective for chronic aches and pains, contracted areas such as stiff neck, upper back, lower back pain, leg muscle tightness, and sore shoulders.

Benefits

  • Relieves chronic muscle pain and tension
  • Breaks up scar tissue and muscle adhesions
  • Reduces inflammation and promotes healing
  • Improves athletic performance and recovery
  • Lowers blood pressure and heart rate
  • Releases muscle knots and trigger points

What to Expect

Your therapist will apply sustained pressure using slow, deep strokes to target the inner layers of your muscles. You may experience some discomfort during the massage as we work through problem areas, but you should never feel pain. Communication with your therapist is key - let them know if the pressure is too intense.

Duration Options

60 Minutes Focus on 1-2 problem areas
90 Minutes Comprehensive full-body deep tissue work

Swedish Massage

The most common type of massage therapy - gentle, relaxing, and perfect for stress relief.

What is Swedish Massage?

Swedish massage is the foundation of Western massage therapy, using five main strokes: long gliding movements (effleurage), kneading (petrissage), rhythmic tapping (tapotement), friction, and vibration. This gentle approach promotes relaxation, eases muscle tension, and creates overall feelings of well-being.

Benefits

  • Promotes deep relaxation and stress relief
  • Improves blood circulation throughout the body
  • Eases muscle tension and minor aches
  • Boosts immune system function
  • Enhances mental clarity and mood
  • Improves sleep quality

What to Expect

You'll relax on a comfortable massage table as your therapist uses oil or lotion to perform smooth, flowing strokes. The pressure is generally light to medium and can be adjusted to your preference. This is an excellent choice if you're new to massage or simply want to unwind.

Duration Options

60 Minutes Perfect for relaxation and stress relief
90 Minutes Extended relaxation with extra attention

Prenatal Massage

Specialized massage therapy designed specifically for the needs of expecting mothers.

What is Prenatal Massage?

Prenatal massage is adapted specifically for the anatomical changes you go through during pregnancy. Our certified therapists are trained in proper positioning and techniques that are safe and beneficial for both mother and baby throughout all stages of pregnancy.

Benefits

  • Reduces back and joint pain
  • Decreases swelling in hands and feet
  • Relieves muscle tension and headaches
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Prepares muscles used during childbirth

What to Expect

We use specially designed cushions and side-lying positions to ensure your comfort and safety throughout the massage. Our therapists avoid certain pressure points and use gentle, nurturing techniques appropriate for pregnancy. We recommend prenatal massage after your first trimester.

Duration Options

60 Minutes Gentle, focused relief for expecting mothers
90 Minutes Extended care and relaxation

Safety First: Please consult with your healthcare provider before booking a prenatal massage, especially if you have any high-risk conditions.

Hot Stone Massage

Therapeutic massage enhanced with heated smooth stones for deep relaxation and healing.

What is Hot Stone Massage?

Hot stone massage combines traditional massage techniques with the use of smooth, heated basalt stones. The heat from the stones penetrates deep into your muscles, allowing your therapist to work more effectively while promoting profound relaxation and stress relief.

Benefits

  • Melts away muscle tension and stiffness
  • Improves blood circulation and lymphatic flow
  • Reduces chronic pain and inflammation
  • Promotes deeper relaxation than standard massage
  • Relieves symptoms of arthritis and fibromyalgia
  • Improves sleep quality

What to Expect

Smooth basalt stones are heated to approximately 130-145°F and placed on key points along your spine, in your palms, and between your toes. Your therapist will also use the stones as an extension of their hands to deliver soothing massage strokes. The warmth is incredibly comforting and therapeutic.

Duration Options

75 Minutes Perfect introduction to hot stone therapy
90 Minutes Full-body treatment with extended relaxation

Thai Combination Massage

The best of both worlds - traditional Thai techniques blended with Swedish massage.

What is Thai Combination Massage?

Our signature Thai Combination massage seamlessly blends the stretching and acupressure of traditional Thai massage with the flowing, relaxing strokes of Swedish massage. This unique approach provides both therapeutic benefits and deep relaxation in one session.

Benefits

  • Combines flexibility benefits with deep relaxation
  • Addresses both muscle tension and stress
  • Improves circulation and energy flow
  • Versatile treatment customized to your needs
  • Perfect for those who can't decide between massage styles
  • Provides comprehensive full-body wellness

What to Expect

Your therapist will consult with you about your preferences and needs, then customize the perfect balance of Thai stretching and Swedish massage techniques. You may start with stretches on the mat, then transition to the table for oil massage, or vice versa depending on your goals.

Duration Options

90 Minutes Ideal time to experience both techniques
120 Minutes Ultimate combination experience
