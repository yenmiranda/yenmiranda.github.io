About Montra Thai Massage

Authentic Thai healing traditions meet modern wellness

Our Story

Montra Thai Massage was born from a deep passion for the ancient healing arts of Thailand and a commitment to bringing authentic wellness to the Houston community.

Founded by practitioners trained in traditional Thai massage techniques, our spa has become a sanctuary for those seeking relief from the stresses of modern life. We believe that true healing comes from honoring time-tested traditions while adapting to meet the unique needs of each client.

The name "Montra" comes from the Thai word meaning "magic spell" or "incantation" – a fitting tribute to the transformative power of therapeutic touch. Every massage at our spa is performed with intention, skill, and the kind of care that can only come from therapists who truly love what they do.

Located in the heart of Houston near West University, we've created a peaceful oasis where ancient wisdom meets contemporary comfort. Our therapists bring years of training and certification from Thailand, ensuring that every treatment you receive is authentic, professional, and deeply healing.
